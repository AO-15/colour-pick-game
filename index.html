How to play

  1. Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
  2. You will see a randomly chosen colour displayed in the "Display colour" box.
  3. Four colour options will appear in the box below.
  4. Choose the colour option that matches the COLOUR and not the text
  5. Your total score and the time it took you to complete the game will be displayed at the end.

    6. Good luck and have fun playing!
